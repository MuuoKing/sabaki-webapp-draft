About Us

WHO WE ARE...

Welcome to AIC Sabaki Church

For over a decade, AIC Sabaki Church has been a beacon of hope and faith in the Athi-River community. We are a vibrant, multicultural congregation committed to spreading the love of Christ and building strong relationships within our community.

Our church family welcomes people from all walks of life, united in our shared faith and commitment to serving God and our neighbors. We believe in the transformative power of God's love and strive to create an environment where everyone can grow spiritually and find their purpose.

OUR FOUNDATION

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture spiritual growth, and serve our community with love, compassion, and integrity while building lasting relationships that reflect God's love.

Our Vision

To be a thriving church community that transforms lives, strengthens families, and impacts our society through the power of God's word and the demonstration of His love.

Our Values

Faith, Love, Unity, Service, Integrity, and Excellence guide everything we do. We believe in authentic worship, biblical teaching, and genuine fellowship.

What to Expect

Planning your first visit? Here's what you can expect when you join us for worship.

Warm Welcome

Our friendly greeters will welcome you at the door and help you find a comfortable seat. Don't worry about feeling out of place - we're all family here.

Experience uplifting contemporary and traditional worship music that will touch your heart and draw you closer to God.

Biblical Teaching

Hear practical, life-changing messages from God's Word that you can apply to your daily life and spiritual journey.
